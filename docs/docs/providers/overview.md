# Provider Overview

Becoming a **Node Provider** on RenderOnNodes allows you to monetize your idle hardware by processing 3D rendering jobs distributed across the network.

While the underlying technology manages the complex orchestration and cryptography, your experience as a Provider focuses on three core actions:

1. **Connecting Hardware:** Linking your GPU to the network via the RenderOnNodes Execution Agent.
2. **Monitoring Operations:** Ensuring your hardware remains online and within optimal thermal limits.
3. **Collecting Earnings:** Tracking your completed jobs and receiving automatic, gas-free payouts in **RON**.

## How the Platform Works for Providers

Unlike traditional cloud services where you rent servers, RenderOnNodes uses a "pull" model. 

When you start your Execution Agent, it signals to the network that you are online. The network's **Distribution Engine** then automatically matches and pushes compute missions to your machine based on your hardware specifications (e.g., VRAM, CUDA core count) and your accumulated trust score.

You do not need to manually accept jobs or communicate with the clients. The platform handles all matching, pricing, and resource delivery automatically.
