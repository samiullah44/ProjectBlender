# Setting up a Wallet

RenderOnNodes does **not use emails or passwords**. Instead, your account is entirely tied to a **Solana Wallet**.

Your wallet acts as your login credential and your bank account simultaneously. If you want to use the platform, you must install one. We highly recommend **Phantom**.

## How to Install Phantom

1. Go to the official [Phantom website](https://phantom.app/) and click **Download** to install the browser extension.
2. Open the extension from your browser toolbar and click **Create a new wallet**.
3. Create a simple password (this just unlocks the extension on your current computer).

## CRITICAL: Your Secret Recovery Phrase

During setup, Phantom will show you a **12-word Secret Recovery Phrase**.

:::danger[DO NOT LOSE THESE 12 WORDS]
**Write these 12 words down on physical paper.**

* If your computer breaks, these 12 words are the ONLY way to recover your money and your RenderOnNodes account.
* **NEVER** give these 12 words to anyone, not even our support team. Anyone who has these 12 words can instantly steal all your money.
:::

## Connecting to RenderOnNodes

Once you have written down your 12 words and finished setting up Phantom:

1. Go to [RenderOnNodes.com](https://app.renderonnodes.com).
2. Click **Connect Wallet** in the top right corner.
3. Select **Phantom**.
4. A popup will ask you to approve the connection. Click **Connect**.

You are now logged in. Your wallet address (a string of letters and numbers like `5J2kBz8...`) is now your permanent account identity on the platform.
