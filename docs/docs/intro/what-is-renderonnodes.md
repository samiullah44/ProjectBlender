# What is RenderOnNodes?

**RenderOnNodes** is a community-driven render farm. Instead of paying a massive corporation to rent server time, you are tapping into a global network of independent computer owners who rent out their idle graphics cards.

We connect **Artists** who need rendering done with **Providers** who have idle hardware, using cryptocurrency to automatically guarantee payments.

---

## How It Practically Works

1. **Upload:** An Artist creates an account and uploads a packed Blender file to our dashboard.
2. **Split:** The platform automatically splits the animation into individual frames.
3. **Render:** The frames are sent to dozens (or hundreds) of Node Providers around the world. Their GPUs render the frames.
4. **Deliver & Pay:** Once a frame is finished and verified, it is automatically sent back to the Artist. The platform automatically deducts the cost from the Artist and pays the Node Provider.

## Why Use It?

### If you are an Artist:
* **Blistering Speed:** Instead of rendering 100 frames on your single computer (which takes 100 hours), you can render 100 frames on 100 different computers on the network simultaneously, finishing in 1 hour.
* **Cheaper Cost:** Providers are rendering from home. They don't have massive corporate server costs, so renders are significantly cheaper than traditional cloud services.

### If you are a Node Provider:
* **Passive Income:** If you have an RTX 3090 sitting idle while you sleep or work, you can turn on the node software and get paid automatically for rendering other people's frames.
